# Tax payer list is empty {#tax_payer_list_is_empty}

At the [еПорези](https://eporezi.purs.gov.rs/) site the tax payer list is empty:

![](<!--path pict-->e_porezi_rs.png){.w-fit .img-frame}

This may probably be common for all users who log in the first time.

The following email correspondence solved this issue (auto translation was used so some
wording may be not perfect). The first email address <eporezi@purs.gov.rs> was found at
<http://www.purs.gov.rs/e-porezi/pitanja.html>:

> **9. Коме могу да се обратим за помоћ у вези електронског подношења пореских пријава?**
> 
> Помоћ се може добити и путем адресе електронске поште путем eporezi@purs.gov.rs.

Probably the address <evidentiranje.stranci@purs.gov.rs> could be used instead from the very
beginning, also see [this FAQ](<!--page taxes-->#pp_gpdg).

was not required and only "štampa od čitača" (the A4 sheet obtained from the card reader)
would be enough.
